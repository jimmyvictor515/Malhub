Welcome to MAL Hub.

Mustapha Akanbi Library Hub is an open, serene and beautiful shared workspace for startups and entrepreneurs at lower cost than they would spend on space of their own. The hub gives small businesses and ambitious entrepreneurs the platform to work and pursue their goals in a revolutionary smart way.

We Create, Build & Develop The Tech Ecosystem

  • Our Vision: To improve the ecosystem through the power of technology.
  • Our Mission: To foster Nigeria's economic growth by providing job for the youths.
  • Our Value: Inexpensive Workspace provision. Community Of Like-Minded Professionals. Programs & Activities for Developer. Job Creation. Cool Atmosphere. Collaboration. Dignity & Respect.

Our Focus

We focus on all SOCIAL ASPECT of the Nigeria economic particularly emphasizing on Job Creation.

Consulting
